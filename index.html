About Us

The IEEE Student Branch at DAIICT ventures to bring awareness among students about the opportunities that exist in the field of engineering. Our goal is to encourage and provide an intellectually exciting university atmosphere for the student body while bridging the gap between industry and students pursuing technology-related subjects. IEEE SB DAIICT seeks to offer the students an interactive platform where they can enhance or acquire their professional and industrial skills while blurring the lines between cliche monotonous and tedious perception of the tech industry and its true immersive and exciting side. Since its establishment, it has been efficient and successful in planning and executing various events with clear aims. With this objective, IEEE SB DAIICT proudly presents its annual Hackathon, Tic-Tech-Toe '24! Tic-Tech-Toe aims to bring creatives and developers together and solve some of the most pressing real-world problems, thus inculcating a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem-solving. The hackathon is open to everyone with a crazy idea and a will to implement it into a working prototype, building an environment of collaborative growth amongst all the participants.
